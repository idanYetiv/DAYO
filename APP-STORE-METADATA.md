### Full Description (4000 chars max)

```
DAYO is the daily companion that helps you plan, reflect, and grow — all in 2 minutes a day.

PLAN YOUR DAY
Start each morning with a clean, focused task list. Add tasks, check them off, and watch your productivity streak grow. No complicated project boards — just what matters today.

JOURNAL YOUR WAY
Write freely or use guided templates. Track your mood with beautiful emoji selections, add photos from your day, note what you're grateful for, and highlight your best moments. DAYO's rich text editor with auto-save means you'll never lose a word.

TRACK YOUR GOALS
Set yearly, monthly, and weekly goals with milestones. Watch your progress bars fill up as you check off milestones. Stay motivated with visual progress tracking.

BUILD BETTER HABITS
Track daily and weekly habits with streak counting. See your consistency at a glance and build routines that stick.

AI-POWERED INSIGHTS
Get gentle writing prompts when you need inspiration. Receive mood-based insights after each diary entry to help you understand your patterns.

SHARE YOUR JOURNEY
Export beautiful diary cards to Instagram Stories or Posts. Choose from multiple templates and styles to share your reflections with the world.

DESIGNED FOR EVERYONE
Switch between Adults Mode (calm, reflective journaling) and Kids Mode (fun, colorful adventure journal) anytime in settings. Perfect for the whole family.

YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL
- Export all your data anytime as JSON
- Delete your account and all data with one tap
- No tracking, no ads, no data selling
- Privacy policy at dayo-web.vercel.app/privacy

FEATURES AT A GLANCE
- Daily task planner with streaks
- Rich diary with mood tracking & photos
- Gratitude journaling (3 daily items)
- Daily highlights with emoji markers
- Goal tracking (yearly/monthly/weekly)
- Habit tracking with streaks
- AI writing companion & mood insights
- Calendar view with mood history
- Diary search with filters
- Instagram export (3 templates)
- Dark mode & custom themes
- Multiple visual themes
- Custom backgrounds
- Push notification reminders
- Kids Mode for young journalers
- RTL language support
- Works offline (coming soon)

Start your journey today. Plan. Reflect. Grow.
```
